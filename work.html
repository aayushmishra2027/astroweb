Access to World Class Facilities

Members of the group are involved in key international space missions such as Astro-H, Astrosat, Cassini, Cluster, INTEGRAL, MMS, NICER, NuStar, Swift and XMM-Newton. We also operate state-of-the-art auroral instrumentation in the Arctic. Members of our group regularly travel to telescopes and reseach groups around the world. The NTT at La Silla is one such example. Rob, Chris and Georgios, from our Supernova Group (http://supernova.soton.ac.uk) explain more in this video.

Fellowships

A high proportion of our staff and postdocs are on prestigious international fellowships, including:

* STFC Ernest Rutherford Fellowships
* Royal Society University Research Fellowships
* Newton International fellowships
* European funding schemes

Please contact the group head if you are considering Southampton hosting you for one of these.
